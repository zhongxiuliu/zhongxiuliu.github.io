Ancient Chinese Literature, Chan(Zen)

I love reading ancient Chinese Literature, especially work from Su Shi. I also believe in Chan(Zen), a branch of Buddahism and philosophy framework that emphasizes on growing wisdom from living daily life instead of conducting religious practices. An example is the "Inner Peace" from Kungfu Panda.
